About Us

I am Sodiq Muritala Lekan, the founder of Success Aso-Oke Heritage Company. My journey into creativity began at an early age in the Kankantu area, where I started as a fine artist. This foundation gave me a deep understanding of colors, design, and artistic expression.

Driven by passion, I ventured into Aso-Oke production as an apprentice under my reputable mentor, Mr. Fatai Aremu Onibon. Under his guidance, I gained valuable skills, experience, and mastery in the craft.

In 2009, I became independent, marking the beginning of Success Aso-Oke Heritage Company. Since then, the brand has grown steadily, built on creativity, quality, and dedication to preserving the rich tradition of Aso-Oke.

Till today, we continue to produce high-quality Aso-Oke fabrics, combining tradition with modern style to meet the needs of our clients for weddings, ceremonies, and special occasions.
